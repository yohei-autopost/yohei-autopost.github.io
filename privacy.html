Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

AI Jiayou ("we", "us", or "our") operates the AI Jiayou application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service, including our integration with TikTok via the TikTok API.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive your TikTok user ID, display name, and profile information as permitted by TikTok's API.
  • Content Data: Videos, captions, and metadata that you authorize us to post on your behalf through TikTok's Content Posting API.
  • Usage Data: Log files, timestamps, and operational data related to the use of our Service (e.g., number of posts, error logs).
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your TikTok account and post content on your behalf as authorized.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To monitor usage and detect/prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.
  • To communicate with you about service updates, errors, or changes to this policy.
  • To comply with applicable laws and TikTok's platform policies.

3. TikTok API and Data Use

Our Service uses the TikTok API to post content on behalf of users who have explicitly authorized such access. We comply with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and API usage policies. Specifically:

  • We only request permissions necessary to post content ("Content Posting API – Direct Post" scope).
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with third parties for advertising or marketing.
  • We store TikTok access tokens securely and revoke them upon user request.
  • We do not use TikTok data to build profiles for advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that assist in operating our platform (e.g., cloud hosting), bound by confidentiality agreements.

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete your account data and revoke all TikTok API tokens associated with your account within 30 days.

6. Data Security
